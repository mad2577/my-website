About the Schemes

The Central Government of India has launched several schemes to promote social welfare, economic growth, and empowerment of its citizens. These schemes target various sectors such as entrepreneurship, women empowerment, financial inclusion, and more. Explore the dropdown menus above to learn about each scheme, visit their official websites, and access detailed information and resources.

  1. Startup India: An initiative to foster innovation and support startups in India.

  2. Beti Bachao Beti Padhao: A campaign to promote the education and welfare of girls.

  3. Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana: A financial inclusion program to provide banking access to all households.

Welcome to the Central Government Schemes page. Here you will find information about various schemes initiated by the government to support and empower citizens across different sectors.
